About Me

I am a Hardware Robotics Engineer passionate about bridging the gap between digital perception and physical interaction. My work focuses heavily on tactile sensing, bio-inspired design, and human-robot interaction.

With a background in Electrical Engineering and Robotics, I enjoy the messy intersection where hardware meets software. I thrive in the lab, whether I'm debugging a noisy signal on an oscilloscope, molding silicone for a soft gripper, or writing ROS nodes to visualize sensor data.

Currently, I am exploring how high-resolution tactile sensors can enable robots to perform dexterous manipulation tasks that were previously impossible.
